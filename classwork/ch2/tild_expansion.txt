tild character (~), matches the current user's home directory. 
example:
harshil@vbox:~/Documents/RHCSA/classwork/ch2$ echo ~root
/root
harshil@vbox:~/Documents/RHCSA/classwork/ch2$ echo ~harshil
/home/harshil
harshil@vbox:~/Documents/RHCSA/classwork/ch2$ echo ~/glob
/home/harshil/glob
harshil@vbox:~/Documents/RHCSA/classwork/ch2$ echo ~nonexistinguser
~nonexistinguser
